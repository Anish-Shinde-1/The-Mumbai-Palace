Food Trivia

What iconic Mumbai street food is often called the "Indian burger"?

The Vada Pav is famously known as the "Indian burger" and is one of Mumbai's most beloved street foods, consisting of a spicy potato fritter sandwiched in a soft bun with chutneys.


Which fiery Mutton curry is known for its deep red color?

The Kolhapuri Mutton Curry is a spicy, bold dish from Maharashtra that gets its signature color and heat from a unique blend of red chilies and roasted spices.
